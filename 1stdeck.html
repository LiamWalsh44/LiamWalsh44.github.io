Deck Instructions

Darkrai/Weavile: The strength of this deck is quick setup for pretty good damage. It has the capbilities to do 90 damage on your first turn getting an energy. You can accomplish this by putting an energy on the darkrai the using dawn to transfer it to the weavile in the active slot to do 90 damage in total with the darkrai passive dealing 20 and the opponents active pokemon having damage buffing weaviles damage to 70. If you get this perfect luck early it is essentially a guaranteed win but even if not with cyrus and other tools this deck can makeup for even the worst of luck and does not take long to learn at all
